1. Introduction

The ADU Construction Progress Dashboard ("Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in via Google OAuth, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture.
  • Session Data: We store your sign-in session in your browser's local storage for convenience.
  • Construction Data: Project progress, expense tracking, and contractor sign-off information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your access to the Application
  • Display personalized construction progress tracking
  • Maintain your session across page reloads
  • Track contractor expense sign-offs
  • Prevent unauthorized access to sensitive project data

4. Data Storage

  • Browser Storage: Your session data is stored locally in your browser only. We do not retain it after you sign out.
  • Backend: Construction and expense data is stored securely on our backend servers.
  • Google: Google handles your authentication. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for their practices.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security audits

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Google (for authentication via OAuth)
  • Project team members with authorized access
  • When required by law or legal process

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent and sign out at any time
  • Request information about how your data is used

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your information, please contact the project administrator.
